How YOU Can Make Porridge!

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Any animal- or plant-sourced milk

Equipment

  • Stove
  • Saucepan
  • Cup
  • Mixing spoon
  • Serving bowl

Instructions

  1. Measure 1 cup of oats and add to the saucepan. Then, measure 2 and a half cups of milk and add to the saucepan.
  2. Set the stove to a medium-high heat and place the saucepan onto it, continuously stirring the mixture with the spoon to prevent sticking.
  3. Once the mixture starts bubbling, set a medium-low heat.
  4. Once the porridge is at the desired consistency, turn off the heat and transfer onto the bowl.
  5. Let it cool down for approximately 5 minutes and enjoy your porridge!
