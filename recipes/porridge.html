How YOU Can Make Porridge!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 2 and a half cups of any animal- or plant-sourced milk

Equipment

  • Cup
  • Saucepan
  • Stove
  • Mixing spoon
  • Serving bowl

Instructions

  1. Measure 1 cup of oats and add to the saucepan. Then, measure 2 and a half cups of milk and add to the saucepan.
  2. Set the stove to a medium-high heat and place the saucepan onto it, regularly stirring the mixture with the spoon to cook evenly and prevent sticking.
  3. Once the mixture starts bubbling, set a medium-low heat.
  4. Once the porridge is at your desired consistency, turn off the heat and pour into the bowl.
  5. Let it cool down for 3-4 minutes and enjoy your porridge!
