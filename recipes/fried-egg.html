How YOU Can Make a Fried Egg!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Equipment

  • Tablespoon
  • Frying pan
  • Stove
  • Spatula
  • Serving plate

Instructions

  1. Use a tablespoon of olive oil to evenly coat the surface of the frying pan.
  2. Set the stove to a medium-high heat and place the frying pan onto it.
  3. Crack the egg into the centre of the frying pan and once the egg white starts bubbling, set a medium-low heat.
  4. Regularly tilt the pan to check and once the egg has become completely solid, turn off the heat and use the spatula to transfer the egg onto the plate.
  5. Let it cool down for 2-3 minutes and enjoy your fried egg!
