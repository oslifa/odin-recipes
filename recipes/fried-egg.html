How YOU Can Make a Fried Egg!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • Olive oil

Equipment

  • Stove
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Serving plate

Instructions

  1. Use a tablespoon of olive oil to evenly coat the surface of the frying pan.
  2. Set the stove to a medium-high heat and place the frying pan onto it.
  3. Crack the egg into the centre of the frying pan and let it cook.
  4. Once the egg white starts bubbling, set a medium-low heat.
  5. Regularly tilt the pan and once the egg is no longer running, turn off the heat and use a spatula to transfer the egg onto the plate.
  6. Let it cool down for approximately 2-3 minutes and enjoy your fried egg!
