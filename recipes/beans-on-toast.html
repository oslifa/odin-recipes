How YOU Can Make Beans on Toast!

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of baked beans
  • 2 slices of bread

Equipment

  • Tin opener
  • Saucepan
  • Stove
  • Mixing spoon
  • Toaster
  • Serving plate

Instructions

  1. Use the tin opener to open the tin of beans and empty into the saucepan.
  2. Set the stove to a medium heat and place the saucepan onto it, regularly stirring the beans with the spoon to cook evenly and prevent sticking.
  3. After 3-4 minutes of cooking, turn off the heat and leave the beans to cool.
  4. Insert the 2 slices of bread into the toaster, set your desired level and toast.
  5. Once the toast is ready, transfer onto the plate. Pour the beans from the saucepan onto the toast and enjoy your beans on toast!
